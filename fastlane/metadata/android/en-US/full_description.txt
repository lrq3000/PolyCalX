PolyCalX is a minimalistic, event-driven calendar widget for Android.

It displays upcoming calendar events in a concise text-based form factor, with per-calendar color coding for people who coordinate multiple agendas. Long-running events are shown once and stay visible while they are ongoing instead of being repeated on every day they span.

Multiple widgets can be added, and each widget keeps its own calendar-selection settings. By default, PolyCalX starts in screenshot/demo mode without calendar access; grant the calendar permission from the widget settings when you want it to read your local calendars.
